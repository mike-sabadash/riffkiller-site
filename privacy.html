Privacy Policy

Riff Killer (“we”) respects your privacy. This page is a placeholder for the full Privacy Policy.

We use Patreon for sign-in and subscription. When you sign in with Patreon, we receive your name, email, and profile image to display in your profile. We do not sell your data.

Local data (e.g. recently practiced riffs, favorites) is stored in your browser (localStorage) and is not sent to our servers except as part of Patreon authentication.

For questions, contact us via the app or Patreon.
